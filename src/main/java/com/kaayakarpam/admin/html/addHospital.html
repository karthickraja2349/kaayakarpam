Registration Guidelines

Please ensure the following:

  • Hospital name should be at least 3 characters long
  • Address should be at least 8 characters long
  • CEO name should contain only alphabets and spaces
  • Phone number must be exactly 10 digits
  • All fields are mandatory for registration
  • Ensure the information provided is accurate and up-to-date
  • Click on the map to set the precise location of your hospital

After submission, our team will verify the details and activate the hospital in our network within 24 hours.
